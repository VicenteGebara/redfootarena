# Third-party assets

RedFoot Arena keeps third-party source assets small and license-scoped. Full downloaded archives should stay out of git; only selected source files and imported Unreal assets are committed.

## Kenney CC0 packs

Source: https://kenney.nl/assets

License: Creative Commons Zero (CC0), also included in each selected pack folder as `License.txt`.

Selected packs:

- Mini Characters: reference character models for future player visuals.
- Mini Arena: banners, columns, gates and trophies for arena decoration.
- Minigolf Kit: colored flags and simple ball/prop references.

Imported Unreal assets live under `Content/External/Kenney`. Source FBX files and licenses live under `ExternalAssets/Kenney`.
